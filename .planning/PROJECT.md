# SD Contractor Agency

## What This Is

A solo agency based in San Diego (Chula Vista) that sells websites and AI receptionists to local contractors — electricians, plumbers, HVAC, and general contractors. Allan pitches in person at businesses with no existing web presence and closes warm leads from a contractor licensing school contact. The business delivers a professionally built contractor website plus an AI-powered phone receptionist, charged as a one-time setup fee plus monthly retainer.

## Core Value

A contractor who answers their own phone and has no website loses jobs every day — this fixes both problems and pays for itself with one extra booked job.

## Context

- **Lead source 1:** Contractor licensing school contact in San Diego — relationship exists, structure not defined yet. Students/grads are in "expand mode" — newly licensed and actively trying to get more jobs, highly motivated buyers.
- **Lead source 2:** Cold in-person visits to contractor businesses with NO website — identified via Google Maps / street research before each outing.
- **Target contractors:** Electricians, plumbers, HVAC, general contractors (not landscapers for now).
- **Geography:** Starting in Chula Vista, routes go south-first then north through San Diego metro.
- **Budget:** $100/month total for tools and subscriptions.
- **Starting point:** Zero assets — no domain, no business name locked, no cards, no accounts yet.
- **First outing:** Tomorrow. Route needs to be planned tonight.
- **Pricing model:** One-time setup (~$1,000–$1,500) + monthly retainer (~$150–$300/mo). Research should validate whether lower entry pricing converts better or if premium positioning wins with contractors (hypothesis: contractors respect premium pricing — they charge it themselves).
- **Website build tool under consideration:** Lovable (lovable.dev) — needs research to confirm if it's the right tool for a repeatable client delivery pipeline vs. a one-off build tool.
- **AI receptionist:** No tool selected yet — research needed (Bland AI, Vapi, Synthflow, GoHighLevel all candidates).

## Constraints

- **Budget:** $100/month max for all tools — must account for hosting, website builder, AI phone platform
- **Solo operator:** Allan is doing sales AND delivery — workflows must be fast and repeatable, not custom each time
- **Timeline:** First sales visit is tomorrow — route planner and sales prep agents are highest priority deliverables
