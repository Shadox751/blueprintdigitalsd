⚡ What NEM 3.0 Means For You

In April 2023, California's Public Utilities Commission changed the rules for rooftop solar. The new "Net Billing Tariff" (NEM 3.0) slashed the credits homeowners receive for sending excess solar power back to the grid — by as much as 75%. This changes how solar pencils out, and most solar companies in San Diego won't tell you the whole story.

The SunRay Advantage: Our certified energy advisors design NEM 3.0-optimized systems that pair solar with battery storage to self-consume as much of your own power as possible. This means your system pays back faster, your savings are higher, and you're protected from grid outages. Don't buy solar from a company that doesn't lead with battery storage — they're leaving your money on the table.
