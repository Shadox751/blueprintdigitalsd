We Only Use Premium Paints

The right paint makes the difference between a job that lasts 2 years and one that lasts 10. We choose quality every time.

Benjamin Moore Aura

PREMIUM INTERIOR — OUR TOP PICK

The gold standard for interior painting. Color Lock technology delivers stunning depth with just two coats — no separate primer needed. Exceptional hide, durability, and a velvet finish that holds up to cleaning.

Is Your Home Pre-1978? We're Lead-Safe Certified.

Homes built before 1978 may contain lead-based paint. Disturbing these surfaces without proper precautions creates health hazards — especially for children and pregnant women.

True Color Painting is fully certified under the EPA RRP (Renovation, Repair & Painting) Lead-Safe program. We follow strict containment, work practice, and cleanup procedures — protecting your family and our crew every step of the way.
