Retaining Wall Experts

RETAINING WALLS
THAT HOLD

San Diego's hilly terrain makes retaining walls one of the most critical improvements a homeowner can make. Hillside erosion costs San Diego homeowners thousands of dollars in property damage. A properly engineered retaining wall protects your property for decades — and adds usable flat space to your yard.

  • Engineered design for your specific hillside load & height
  • Stamped and decorative finishes available on poured walls
  • Drainage systems built in — gravel backfill + perforated pipe
  • County permit assistance handled by our team
  • Block, poured concrete, and combination systems available
